Medical Technology

Capabilities

Medical devices are crucial to the success of the Medical field, which is why Jenkinson Enterprises innovates solutions to improve them. Virtual and Augmented Reality are very important for scoping out the situation and planning for a medical treatment. It can be used to map out a patient's brain, show diagnoses, and even simulate a real surgery without the doctor being in the surgery room. The accuracy and efficieny of this technology is important, so we make sure maximize them while customizing the capabilites of the technology to our client's needs

Robotic Surgery has been helping doctors perform countless surgeries that need precision. Jenkinson Enterprises prioritizes designing and engineering solutions that pushes the boundaries of robotics' potential in the medical field.

We also develop and maintain Nanotechnology. Specifcially for the medical field, we develop nanoparticles for drug delivery systems, medical imaging, scaffolding for tissue regenration and many more uses.
